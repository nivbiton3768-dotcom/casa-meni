# Product Brief: Casa Meni

## Executive Summary

Casa Meni is an all-in-one property management platform built for small-to-mid real estate operators who do it all — buy, renovate, rent (short-term and long-term), maintain, and sell properties. Today, these operators stitch together 5-7 disconnected tools: AppFolio for tenant management, Guesty for Airbnb operations, DealCheck for deal analysis, Stessa for investor reporting, Buildertrend for renovation tracking, and QuickBooks for accounting. Each tool owns a slice of the data, none of them talk to each other, and the operator lives in spreadsheet hell trying to see the full picture.

Casa Meni replaces the entire stack with a single platform where every dollar, every work order, every lease, every renovation photo, and every investor report lives in one system. Because all the data is unified, Casa Meni unlocks features that are structurally impossible for point solutions — scoring acquisition deals against your own historical renovation costs, auto-generating a seller's package from years of ownership data, or showing an investor their real-time returns across a mixed short-term and long-term portfolio.

The platform serves every stakeholder through role-based access: the owner sees a command center dashboard, tenants pay rent and submit maintenance requests, technicians receive GPS-routed work orders, cleaning teams get auto-scheduled tasks from booking calendars, investors track live returns and distributions, and vendors submit invoices while building reputation scores. One platform, every role, the full property lifecycle.

## The Problem

Small-to-mid property operators (5-50 units) who buy, renovate, and manage properties face a brutal software fragmentation problem. The property management software market exceeds $26B and is growing at 8%+ annually, but it's built for specialists — Buildium and AppFolio serve long-term rental managers, Guesty and Hostaway serve short-term rental operators, DealCheck and PropStream serve acquisition analysts, Stessa serves investor bookkeepers, and Buildertrend serves contractors. No single tool serves the operator who does ALL of these things.

The consequences are painful and measurable:

- **Data lives in silos.** Renovation costs are in one tool, rental income in another, investor distributions in a spreadsheet. There's no single source of truth for property-level P&L, and operators spend 10-15 hours/month manually reconciling data between systems.
- **Insights are impossible.** "Which renovation type gives the best ROI?" "Should I convert this Airbnb to a long-term lease?" "What's my true all-in cost per unit?" These questions require data from 3+ systems, and nobody has time to build the reports.
- **Team coordination fragments.** Maintenance techs use one app, cleaners use another, tenants email, investors call. Nothing is documented in one place. Communication gaps are where expensive problems hide.
- **Investor reporting is manual and unprofessional.** Operators building portfolios with investor capital lose credibility (and future investment) when they send quarterly Excel reports instead of offering a live investor portal.
- **Growth is punished.** Every new property means another account in every tool, more manual data entry, more reconciliation. Operational overhead scales faster than the portfolio.

## The Solution

Casa Meni is a unified platform covering the full real estate lifecycle — from deal sourcing through property disposition — with role-based portals for every stakeholder.

**For the operator:** A command center dashboard showing portfolio health at a glance — cash flow, occupancy, maintenance load, renovation progress, and investor returns. AI-prioritized daily action feeds. Real-time P&L per property with drill-down to individual transactions. Deal pipeline management. Renovation war rooms with budget-vs-actual tracking. Smart alerts with configurable escalation rules.

**For tenants:** One-tap rent payment (ACH, card, Zelle, Apple Pay), photo-based maintenance requests with Uber-style technician tracking, digital lease vault, communication hub with emergency escalation, and move-in/move-out photo inspection wizards.

**For field teams:** Maintenance technicians get prioritized work orders with GPS routing, barcode scanning for parts expense tracking, offline mode, and labor time logging. Cleaning teams get auto-scheduled tasks triggered by booking check-outs, room-by-room photo checklists, and supply inventory tracking.

**For investors:** A live returns portal showing IRR, cash-on-cash, equity multiples, and distribution history. Full P&L transparency per property. Automated K-1 generation. Capital call workflows for new deals.

**For vendors:** Job notifications with scope and photos, in-app invoicing, payment tracking, and a performance reputation system that rewards quality with preferred status.

## What Makes This Different

**1. Unified data creates impossible features.** Because acquisition costs, renovation expenses, rental income, maintenance spend, and sale proceeds all live in one system, Casa Meni offers features that are structurally impossible for any combination of point solutions — deal scoring based on your own renovation cost history, auto-generated seller packages from years of ownership data, STR-to-LTR conversion calculators using actual numbers, and renovation ROI tracking by component type.

**2. Full lifecycle coverage.** No competitor spans buy → renovate → rent → maintain → sell. Buildium stops at "manage." Guesty stops at "host." DealCheck stops at "analyze." Casa Meni is the first platform built for operators who do the entire cycle.

**3. The compounding data moat.** Every property managed on Casa Meni generates operational intelligence that makes every future decision sharper — cost-per-square-foot databases by renovation type, tenant quality scores, seasonal pricing patterns, vendor reliability rankings. This data compounds in value over time and cannot be replicated by tools that see only one slice of the business. After 2-3 years of operation, an operator's Casa Meni dataset becomes one of their most valuable business assets.

**4. Professional investor experience attracts capital.** Operators who present live dashboards, automated K-1s, and transparent P&Ls raise more capital than those sending quarterly PDFs. Casa Meni isn't just a management tool — it's a capital-raising platform. Look more professional → attract more investors → grow faster → manage more properties → generate more data.

## Who This Serves

**Primary: The Owner-Operator (5-50 units, mixed portfolio)**
Runs a property business spanning multiple activities — acquiring, renovating, renting, maintaining, selling. Currently drowning in tool fragmentation. Values efficiency and data-driven decisions. Often manages a mix of short-term and long-term rentals. May have 2-10 investors and a small field team (1-3 maintenance techs, 2-5 cleaners).

**Ideal early adopter:** Operators managing BOTH short-term and long-term rentals who also buy/renovate properties. They suffer the worst fragmentation pain and gain the most from unification.

**Secondary stakeholders (served through role-based access):**
- Long-term tenants, short-term rental guests
- Maintenance technicians, cleaning teams
- Investors and capital partners
- Vendors, contractors, and subcontractors
- Accountants and bookkeepers
- Property inspectors

## Success Criteria

- **Operator time saved:** Reduce manual reconciliation and cross-tool management by 10+ hours/month within 60 days of onboarding
- **Portfolio visibility:** 100% of properties with real-time P&L within 30 days
- **Stakeholder adoption:** 80%+ of tenants actively using portal within 60 days
- **Maintenance efficiency:** 30% reduction in average work order resolution time within 90 days
- **Investor satisfaction:** Zero manual report generation — 100% automated distributions and reporting
- **Retention:** 90%+ annual retention rate, measured by continued active usage

## Scope

**V1 (MVP) — The Foundation:**
- Financial engine (real-time P&L per property, bank feed integration with AI categorization, multi-entity/LLC support, chart of accounts)
- Command center dashboard (portfolio overview, daily action feed, smart alerts)
- Tenant portal (rent collection with multiple payment methods, maintenance requests with photo/video, digital lease vault, communication hub)
- Maintenance work order system (assignment, priority queue, photo documentation, time tracking)
- Basic investor portal (returns view, document sharing, distribution tracking)
- Property and unit database with inspection templates

**V1 Migration Strategy:** CSV import from Buildium/AppFolio/Stessa, bank feed setup wizard, guided onboarding for first 5 properties with white-glove support for early adopters.

**V2 — Full Lifecycle:**
- Short-term rental operations (channel management, cleaning automation, guest communication, dynamic pricing)
- Renovation project management (budget tracking, contractor progress, material costs)
- Deal pipeline and acquisition tools (CRM, deal scoring, due diligence checklists)

**V3 — Intelligence Layer:**
- AI business advisor, predictive maintenance, market intelligence
- Direct booking website builder
- Vendor/cleaning marketplace
- White-label platform

## Vision

If Casa Meni succeeds, it becomes the operating system for real estate entrepreneurs. Year one: replace the fragmented tool stack for mixed-portfolio owner-operators. Year two: expand into short-term rental operations, renovation management, and acquisition pipeline tools, completing the full lifecycle. Year three: the aggregated data across operators enables market intelligence, AI-powered business advisory, and a platform play where the best contractors, cleaners, and service providers are matched to properties through a managed marketplace.

The long-term moat is data. Every property managed on Casa Meni generates operational intelligence that makes the next decision better — for that operator and, eventually, for the network. The end state is a platform where buying, renovating, renting, maintaining, and selling properties all happen through a single system that gets smarter with every transaction. Casa Meni doesn't just manage properties — it makes property businesses more intelligent.
