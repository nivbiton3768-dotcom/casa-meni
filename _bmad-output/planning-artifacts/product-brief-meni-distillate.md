# Casa Meni — Product Brief Distillate

## Feature Universe (101 ideas from brainstorming, organized by pillar)

### Pillar 1: Command & Control Dashboard
- Command center showing portfolio value, cash flow, occupancy, overdue rents, maintenance tickets, renovation progress, lease expirations — color-coded green/yellow/red
- AI-generated daily action feed that reprioritizes every morning
- Portfolio heat map — map view with color-coded pins, filterable by occupancy, cash flow, maintenance load
- Fintech-grade UX (inspired by Mint/Robinhood) with swipe, tap, drill-down
- AI Business Advisor — monthly insight reports with optimization recommendations
- Vacancy loss calculator — track daily cost of vacant units

### Pillar 2: Financial Engine
- Real-time P&L per property (monthly/quarterly/yearly/lifetime), side-by-side comparison
- Full double-entry chart of accounts pre-configured for real estate
- Bank feed integration with AI auto-categorization that learns vendor-property associations
- Multi-entity/LLC support — separate P&L/balance sheets per LLC plus consolidated view
- Accounts payable automation — invoice photo → AI extraction → routing → payment → ledger
- Bank reconciliation wizard with trust account compliance (legally required in most states)
- Unified payment infrastructure (Stripe-style) — rent in, vendor payments out, distributions, refunds
- Auto-import via Plaid — mortgage payments auto-split principal/interest
- Tax autopilot — Schedule E categorization, depreciation tracking, cost segregation, 1031 exchange flags, mileage tracker
- 1099 generation and e-filing for all vendors/contractors

### Pillar 3: Tenant Experience
- One-tap rent payment — ACH, card, Zelle, Apple Pay. Auto-pay, roommate split, late fee preview
- Maintenance requests with photo/video, urgency categorization, real-time status, post-job rating
- Digital lease vault — lease, addenda, move-in photos, pet agreements, parking. E-sign renewals
- Communication hub — in-app messaging, read receipts, searchable history, emergency escalation button
- Community board — announcements, package notifications, amenity booking, event calendar
- Move-in/move-out wizard — room-by-room photo checklist, dual sign-off, deposit deduction with evidence
- Payment plan requests for tenants in financial difficulty — reduces evictions
- Renter's insurance integration — in-app quotes, policy tracking, expiration reminders
- Tenant quality score — payment history, request frequency, compliance tracking

### Pillar 4: Maintenance & Field Operations
- Work order queue sorted by priority with property info, tenant photos, access instructions, part history per unit
- GPS navigation with multi-job route optimization, "on my way" auto-notification (Uber-style)
- Photo before/after with annotation, auto-timestamp AND GPS-stamp (proof of location)
- Barcode scanner for parts — scan Home Depot receipt, auto-allocate cost to property
- Time tracking per job — separate travel vs work time, auto-calculate labor cost, weekly timesheet
- Offline mode — full functionality without connectivity, auto-sync
- Preventive maintenance scheduler — recurring tasks by property and season (HVAC, smoke detectors, gutters)
- Inspection-to-work-order pipeline — one tap from inspection finding to pre-populated work order
- Real-time technician tracking visible to tenant (Uber-style map)
- Predictive maintenance via IoT sensors (future — HVAC, water heater anomaly detection)
- Utility management — track all utilities, auto-pay/bill-back, usage anomaly detection (leak alerts)

### Pillar 5: Short-Term Rental Operations
- Auto-scheduled cleaning tasks triggered by guest checkout, with "RUSH" flag for back-to-back
- Room-by-room checklist with photo verification for remote manager sign-off
- Supply inventory per property — usage logging, low-stock alerts, auto-generated shopping lists
- Damage report pipeline — cleaning team finds damage → maintenance ticket → guest charge, one flow
- Cleaning team earnings dashboard — completed jobs, pay, tips, 1099 export
- Digital welcome guide — interactive, branded, live-updating (WiFi, codes, rules, local recs)
- Smart lock code delivery — unique per guest, role-based codes for cleaners/techs, time-windowed
- In-stay guest issue reporting direct to maintenance pipeline
- Upsell marketplace — early check-in, late checkout, airport pickup, grocery pre-stocking
- Review prompt with incentive — discount code for 5-star reviews, AI auto-response
- RevPAR/ADR/occupancy analytics, STR-to-LTR conversion calculator
- AI guest concierge — multilingual chatbot handling 80% of questions, property-specific knowledge
- Dynamic pricing engine — auto-adjust rates based on events, seasonality, YOUR booking data
- Guest behavior insights — channel performance, stay patterns, damage frequency
- On-demand cleaning marketplace (DoorDash-style) — post to verified cleaners, surge pricing
- Direct booking website builder (Shopify-style) — brand, promo codes, returning guest discounts

### Pillar 6: Acquisition Pipeline
- Deal pipeline Kanban — Lead → Analyzing → Under Contract → Due Diligence → Closing → Owned
- CRM for property leads — source tracking, follow-up reminders, visit notes
- Comp analysis tool using YOUR historical renovation cost data for ARV estimates
- Closing checklist and document tracker with deadline alerts
- AI deal scoring 1-100 based on YOUR portfolio performance history
- Due diligence checklist engine with contingency deadline alerts
- AI deal recommendations for investors (Netflix-style) based on investment history

### Pillar 7: Investor Relations
- One-click branded PDF investor reports — auto-generated, scheduled monthly
- Investor portal — total invested, value, CoC, IRR, equity multiple, distributions, interactive charts
- Distribution tracker — every distribution with date, amount, source, type. K-1 auto-generation
- Full P&L transparency per invested property (not sanitized summaries)
- Investor document vault — operating agreements, closing docs, insurance, appraisals. Versioned
- Capital call workflow — send in-app, investors review deal package, commit, wire, track funding

### Pillar 8: Renovation Management
- Renovation war room — budget vs actual per property, phase completion, contractor schedules, photo timeline, permit status, change orders. 10% budget overrun alerts
- Cost-per-square-foot historical database — "kitchens avg $85/sqft in our market"
- Contractor progress portal — daily photo updates, milestone-based payment triggers
- Material price watcher — alerts for drops at Home Depot, Lowe's, suppliers
- ROI tracker per renovation component — data-driven prioritization (kitchens 23% ROI vs bathrooms 12%)
- Visual renovation planning on floor plans (Figma-style) — drag fixtures, share with contractors
- Full project management with Gantt charts, dependencies, critical path (Asana-style)

### Pillar 9: Vendor Ecosystem
- Job notification and acceptance — scope, photos, address, urgency. Accept/decline, estimate, approval
- Invoice submission and payment tracking — upload or generate, track approval, payment history, 1099
- Performance reputation system — response time, quality ratings, completion rate. Preferred status

### Pillar 10: Property Disposition
- Disposition manager — pull full financial history, generate seller's package, list, track inquiries, manage offers
- Buyer inquiry pipeline (seller-side CRM)
- Auto-generated seller package from full ownership history — P&L, rent history, maintenance log, renovation costs, photos, cap rate
- AI property valuation using YOUR financial data plus market data (better than Zestimate)

### Pillar 11: Communication
- Smart alerts and escalation engine — configurable rules with auto-actions and chains
- Property-centric team channels (Slack-style) — threads, @mentions, search, file sharing
- Multi-language support — English, Spanish, Hebrew
- Emergency protocol system — one-button crisis mode with pre-configured playbooks

### Pillar 12: Platform Infrastructure
- Inspection template builder — move-in, move-out, annual, pre/post-renovation
- Side-by-side photo comparison (move-in vs current) — legal-grade documentation
- Rent roll with escalation modeling — vacancy risk factored into rent increase decisions
- Lease renewal automation — auto comps → suggest rent → send offer → track → trigger vacancy prep
- Compliance vault — jurisdiction-specific document tracking with auto-renewal reminders
- One-click supply reordering (Amazon-style) with recurring auto-orders
- Every workflow as visual Kanban pipeline (Salesforce-style)
- Document version control (GitHub-style) — diff view for lease changes
- Self-service showing scheduler (Calendly-style) for vacant units
- Per-property knowledge base wiki (Notion-style) — appliance manuals, contractor contacts, quirks
- Insurance claim manager — start claims in-app with all documentation already in system
- Legal document generator — AI-powered, jurisdiction-aware leases and notices
- White-label SaaS option for other property managers
- Mentor/apprentice training mode — restricted view with guided workflows

| Maintenance Tech | Work-order scoped | View/update work orders, log time, scan parts |

| Vendor/Contractor | Job-scoped | Accept jobs, submit invoices, track payments |
| Guest (STR) | Booking-scoped | Welcome guide, issue reporting, upsells |

| Inspector | Inspection-scoped | Templates, photo documentation, work order creation |

## Technical Preferences & Constraints (captured during brainstorming)
- Mobile-first design critical for field teams (maintenance, cleaning, inspections)
- Offline mode required for technicians working in basements/remote areas
- Smart lock integration needed (August, Schlage, Yale)
- Bank feed integration via Plaid
- Payment processing likely via Stripe
- Channel management APIs (Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com) needed for STR
- Barcode/QR scanning for parts inventory
- GPS for technician routing and photo stamping

## Open Questions
- Pricing model not yet defined (per-unit? per-user? tiered? transaction fees?)
- Technology stack not yet chosen (web framework, mobile approach — native vs cross-platform)
- Data migration strategy from existing tools needs detailed planning
- Regulatory compliance requirements vary by state/jurisdiction — scope of legal template coverage?
- Insurance and liability considerations for marketplace features (cleaning, vendor)
- Whether to build accounting engine from scratch vs integrate with existing (e.g., QuickBooks API)

## Scope Signals (MVP Boundaries)
- **IN for V1:** Financial engine, dashboard, tenant portal, maintenance, basic investor portal
- **V2:** STR operations, renovation management, acquisition pipeline
- **V3:** AI features, marketplace, white-label
- **Explicitly NEVER:** Becoming a property listing marketplace or competing with Zillow/Redfin on consumer search
